VISION

To create a badminton community that is supportive, progressive, friendly, competitive and encouraging. To ensure that athletes, no matter what their age or background, will have an opportunity to reach their potential.

MISSION

To promote participation in badminton as a sport for life and encourage the growth of badminton athletes, coaches and officials throughout MBA The mission of BSAB is to promote camaraderie, friendship and development of fitness and health a peaceful and positive quality of life through Badminton as sport.

ABOUT BADMINTON

Badminton was invented long ago; its origins date back at least two thousand years to the game of battledore and shuttlecock played in ancient Greece, India and China. Badminton took its name from Badminton House in Gloucestershire, the home of the Duke of Beaufort, where the sport was played in the last century. By coincidence, Gloucestershire is now the base for the International Badminton Federation. Founded in 1934 with nine members - Canada, Denmark, England, France, Ireland, Netherlands, New Zealand, Scotland and Wales, membership of the International Badminton Federation has risen steadily.
